HOW TO PLAY: Click on empty space to grow a new circle. If the new circle touches an existing circle of same color, they both disappear. If they have different colors, both stay. If the new circle cannot fit, a minimal dotted outline is shown. After 3 such misfits in a row, the game is over. If you click inside a standalone circle, it will grow more.
